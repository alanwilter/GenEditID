Target Coverage Metrics

Target coverage metrics were computed using Picard CollectTargetedPcrMetrics.

In computing target coverage metrics, Picard CollectTargetedPcrMetrics does not include reads that are unmapped or have a mapping quality of zero (indicating that they align equally well to more than one location); it also excludes supplemental records, e.g. for chimeric (split read) alignments from bwa mem.
